Vertical Pull Progression

View Progression
  • 1. Dead Hangs – Hang from a bar to build grip strength.
  • 2. Scapular Pulls – Activate your lats by retracting your scapula while hanging.
  • 3. Assisted Pull-Ups – Use bands or a chair to perform pull-ups.
  • 4. Negative Pull-Ups – Jump up and slowly lower yourself.
  • 5. Chin-Ups – Underhand grip to engage biceps more.
  • 6. Pull-Ups – Standard shoulder-width overhand grip.
  • 7. Wide Pull-Ups – Increase lat emphasis and difficulty.
  • 8. L-Sit Pull-Ups – Add core activation to your pull-ups.
  • 9. Archer Pull-Ups – One side works harder, builds unilateral strength.
  • 10. One Arm Pull-Ups – Elite level bodyweight mastery.
