Minimal Gear

  • Pull-up Bar: Essential for upper-body pulling exercises like pull-ups, chin-ups, and muscle-ups. It builds upper-body strength and allows for progressions leading to advanced movements like muscle-ups.
  • Dip Bars or Parallel Bars: Crucial for developing pushing strength in the chest, shoulders, and triceps. Dips can be advanced with the addition of weight or ring variations.
  • Resistance Bands: Perfect for assisting with difficult movements like pull-ups and dips or providing resistance during mobility work to help improve flexibility and strength.
  • Ab Wheel: (Optional) A powerful tool for building core strength and stability, helping improve body control during various calisthenics movements.
  • Rings: For advanced skill work and stabilization, rings engage more muscle groups than bars due to their instability, allowing for advanced exercises like ring dips, muscle-ups, and front lever work.
  • Weighted Vest: (Optional) Adds resistance to bodyweight exercises to increase intensity and stimulate muscle growth.
  • Yoga Mat or Padded Surface: Important for floor work, ensuring comfort and safety during exercises like push-ups, planks, and core exercises.

Daily Workouts

Train daily with rotating focuses or combine skill + strength work each session:

  • Skill + Strength Day: Practice a high-level move (e.g., handstand or front lever), then hit basics: pushups, dips, pullups, squats.
  • Full Body: Circuit: Pullups, Pushups, Dips, Squats, Leg Raises x 3–5 rounds.
  • Max Effort: One progression per move, taken to failure. 3 rounds.
  • Explosive Day: Clapping pushups, jump squats, muscle-up drills, explosive dips.
  • Recovery/Mobility: Light flow, bridges, stretches, and band mobility work.
