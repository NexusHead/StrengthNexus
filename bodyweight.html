Bodyweight Mastery

Where you can get strong anywhere and anytime using nothing but your body and discipline.

What Is Bodyweight Mastery?

It is the ability to control, manipulate, and master your own body in space. Strength, mobility, and skill come together as one. This section covers detailed progressions and the gear you need to unlock your body’s true potential.

Exercise Progressions

Vertical Pull

1. Dead Hang – Learn to hang and decompress the shoulders.

2. Active Hang – Engage the scapula while hanging.

3. Jumping Pull-Up – Use your legs to assist the pull.

4. Negative Pull-Up – Slowly lower from the top position.

5. Pull-Up – Standard vertical pulling strength.

6. L-Sit Pull-Up – Engage core while pulling.

7. Archer Pull-Up – Emphasize unilateral strength.

8. Typewriter Pull-Up – Move side to side at the top.

9. Chest-to-Bar Pull-Up – Pull higher for upper back engagement.

10. One-Arm Pull-Up – The peak of vertical pulling mastery.

Horizontal Pull

1. Table Rows – Beginner horizontal pulling.

2. Elevated Rows – Increase the difficulty by raising feet.

3. Ring Rows – Add instability for more challenge.

4. Archer Rows – Work one side more than the other.

5. Inverted Rows – Flat body horizontal pulling.

6. Feet-Elevated Rows – Harder angle.

7. Wide Grip Rows – Emphasize rear delts.

8. Weighted Rows – Add resistance.

9. False Grip Rows – Carryover to rings.

10. One-Arm Rows – Maximum control and strength.

Essential Gear

Pull-Up Bar

The foundation of vertical pulling strength and static holds.

Dip Bars or Parallettes

Used for push exercises, dips, and deep range support work.

Resistance Bands

Assist difficult movements and build control.

Weighted Vest

Great for progressive overload once bodyweight is too easy.

Starter Programs (3–6 Days/Week)

3-Day Foundation Builder

Focuses on basic push, pull, and core skills. Perfect for total beginners.

4-Day Skill Split

Upper/lower split with statics and mobility work.

5-Day Mastery Grind

High-volume training with progressive overload and movement focus.

6-Day Elite Warrior Routine

Maximizes volume and skill acquisition with optional mobility Sundays.
